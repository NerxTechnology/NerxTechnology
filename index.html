Game Jams

Small, free games made for game jams over the course of a weekend to a month. All hosted on itch at https://nerxtech.itch.io. The programming was done by me for all of these, as well as some of the art.

Planets in 20

Created for the 20s Game jam. There can only be 20 seconds of gameplay in the game, no carry over from previous plays (ex. no upgrades), and easy restart. I had planned to contribute to the Proc Gen Jam that happened before this one so I was learning about procedural generation, especially for world generation. I ended up putting that knowledge into the 20s jam. Mobile or Desktop.
